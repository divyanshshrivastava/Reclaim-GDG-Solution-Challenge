About Reclaim

Transforming waste management through technology and community action.

Our Mission

At Reclaim, we're driven by a simple yet powerful vision: to transform waste from a global crisis into a valuable resource through innovative technology and community action.

We believe that every discarded item has potential. By leveraging AI and community knowledge, we're building a future where waste becomes worth.

" We don't just recycle. We reimagine, reinvent, and reclaim what others discard. "
Contact Us Try Our AI Analysis

Our Story

01

The Beginning

Reclaim started as a student project at a hackathon focused on sustainability challenges. The founding team was shocked by statistics showing how much valuable material ends up in landfills.

02

The Innovation

The team developed a prototype AI system that could identify materials in waste items and suggest creative reuse ideas. This prototype won first place and secured initial funding.

03

Today's Impact

Now powered by Google's Gemini AI, Reclaim has grown into a global community with thousands of users sharing upcycling ideas and diverting tons of waste from landfills every month.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI waste analysis?

Our AI model has a 95% accuracy rate for identifying common household materials and providing relevant upcycling suggestions.

Is Reclaim available worldwide?

Yes! Reclaim is accessible globally. While our marketplace shipping is currently limited to certain regions, all other features are available worldwide.

Can I sell my upcycled creations on Reclaim?

Absolutely! Once you create an account, you can set up your shop profile and start listing your sustainable creations in our marketplace.

How does Reclaim contribute to sustainability?

By helping users repurpose items that would otherwise go to landfills, we've helped divert over 25 tons of waste in our first year alone.
